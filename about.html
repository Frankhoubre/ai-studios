AI Studios

AI Studios is a private French-speaking community and training platform for AI-powered creators — focused on video creation, storytelling, and creative business.

AI video Storytelling Prompts Creative business French training

Vision

Make AI creation accessible, structured, and professional — so creators can turn imagination into real, high-quality content and sustainable projects.

AI Studios promotes a creator-first approach: skills, craft, taste, and direction — not shortcuts.

Mission

Provide a step-by-step French learning path and a supportive community, helping members:

  • create premium videos (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) with AI
  • build cinematic visuals (image generation, animation workflows)
  • develop storytelling and narrative structure
  • use AI voice, music, and sound design for production-quality results
  • package offers, price services, and acquire clients
  • ship real projects consistently (portfolio, brand, monetization)

Who it’s for

  • content creators and filmmakers
  • freelancers, editors, and creative entrepreneurs
  • people starting from scratch (no technical background required)
  • anyone who wants repeatable workflows, not random experimentation
